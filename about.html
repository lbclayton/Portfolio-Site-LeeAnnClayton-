My Story

After graduating my college design program, I gained practical experience working as a professional graphic design intern at Disney Parks, Experience & Products. There, I worked with the Creative Services team on various projects, spanning different areas of design.

I decided to pursue a product design career after working in digital marketing for various freelance clients. I enjoyed connecting with audiences through creative social media campaigns and seeing the direct results of my work from analytics. After stumbling across a product design position and reading its description I realized it was the perfect career for me and looked for ways to learn more.

I decided to enroll in a UI immersion course with CareerFoundry so I would be better prepared for a product design role. There, I gained knowledge in user experience research and design, user interface design and specialized in front-end web development.

Along the way I have worked with various clients at junior graphic design level, helping to build brands and create awesome websites.
