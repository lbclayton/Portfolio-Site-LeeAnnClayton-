INTRO

Streaming services revolutionized how the world consumes their favorite entertainment media by providing an exclusive platform to watch titles whenever and wherever they want. But these platforms do not offer a sense of community within the product. Fans of their content must find community elsewhere. Scattering the fanbase and losing potential audiences for new shows. By focusing on the Sci-Fi genre, Dimensions will centralize the sci-fi fanbase to be an all-in-one platform for streaming genre content and facilitating discussion. On top of being a streaming service focusing on a specific genre it will also partner with film festivals and live events and screen new films in the genre. Dimensions aims to bridge the gap between content streaming and fan discussion.
